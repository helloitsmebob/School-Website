Grade Levels

Preschool

Our Preschool Curriculum consists of six components: 1. Core Knowledge Preschool Sequence 2. Capturing Kids’ Hearts 3. Saxon Math Preparation 4. Handwriting Without Tears 5. Zoo-phonics 6. Stop and Think

Welcome to SkyView Academy Preschool! We are so excited you are considering our Preschool in Highlands Ranch, as a place for your child to grow and learn and look forward to partnering with you in the early stages of your child’s education. Our tuition-based preschool program in Highlands Ranch is dedicated to providing a high quality, developmentally appropriate, well-rounded learning experience for each child in a safe, nurturing environment. Our curriculum is based on the Core Knowledge Sequence (also used in grades K-8) which outlines a cumulative and coherent progression of knowledge and skills for our early learners.



Elemantary School

Our program includes: 1. Engaging teachers who care about and support our students 2. Core Knowledge curriculum with well-aligned reading, writing, math and science programs 3. Rotating specials which include instruction in computers, art, music, and P.E. for all students 4. Spanish instruction for all PreK through 5th-grade students every day 5. Character programs through Capturing Kids’ Hearts and Character First 6. Service learning opportunities 7. After school clubs and activities including music, arts, science, and physical education

Welcome to SkyView Academy Elementary School! I count it an honor to serve our elementary school program. Serving this charter school community allows our staff to invest in our local community. SkyView Academy prioritizes a commitment to learning. However, most importantly, SkyView Academy celebrates the value of each individual child. SkyView Academy Elementary School provides students with a well-balanced learning environment. Our staff empowers their learning through research-based instruction. Additionally, students explore and engage in problem-solving skills. They strive to act responsibly within their charter school community. As a result, both the younger and older students in our elementary school care for the physical world: locally, regionally, and globally. Most importantly, our students engage in a culture of respect.



Middle School

Highlights of our program include: 1. Core Knowledge curriculum with well-aligned reading, writing, math and science programs along with daily taught Spanish classes for all students 2. Electives including P.E., yearbook, drama, choir, art, technology and leadership 3. Engaging teachers who care about and support our students 4. Character program through Capturing Kids’ Hearts and service learning projects 5. Leadership opportunities and class spirit 6. Competitive middle school athletic program 7. Individual student lockers (a big deal when you’re in middle school!)

Welcome to SkyView Academy Middle School! I feel absolutely honored to serve as our middle school principal. I thoroughly enjoy kids at this uncertain age. Engaging with them, helping them find their way, and encouraging them, brings me unspeakable joy. I know, crazy, right?!

High School

It is an honor and privilege to lead the high school at SkyView Academy. We truly enjoy building relationships with our students as we work to guide and mentor them in preparation for college and life beyond SkyView. It’s not often that you actually have the opportunity to have positive, meaningful relationships with your high school principals, but we sincerely love talking with our students, learning about their lives, and supporting them in any way we can!

At SkyView, our goal is to see 100% of our graduates prepared for life during and beyond high school. As such, we provide a rigorous, engaging, safe environment which prepares graduates for success. By utilizing a college prep, liberal arts curriculum and emphasizing a classical approach to education, we prepare our students to both think critically and become lifelong learners. Throughout their journey students experience a culture of high expectations along with high levels of social, emotional, and academic support.

News/Events

  • SkyView Academy Theater Presents Newsies the Musical

    Based on the real-life Newsboy Strike of 1899, this new Disney musical tells the story of Jack Kelly, a rebellious newsboy who dreams of a life as an artist away from the big city. After publishing giant Joseph Pulitzer raises newspaper prices at the newsboys’ expense, Kelly and his fellow newsies take action. With help from the beautiful female reporter Katherine Plumber, all of New York City soon recognizes the power of “the little man.”

  • SkyView Academy Wish Week 2021

    Starting on Monday 4/5, SkyView Academy will begin celebrating our 3rd Wish Week. We are excited to honor this year’s Wish Kid, Kian from Greenwood Village. Throughout this week-long event, we partner with Make-A-Wish Colorado and their “Kids For Wish Kids” program to feature a child with critical illnesses. We are so excited to see our community come together as we work to raise $5,000 and grant wishes for kids like Kian. Kian is currently waiting for his wish to be granted! This is a special opportunity for us to demonstrate honorable leadership through service. Though this Wish Week will look different than in past years, there are several opportunities for K-12 students and families to get involved and make this week special. Many of these will be virtual this year with our own digital fundraising page to contribute to this great cause!
